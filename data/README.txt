the data folder contain the model file, texture images and calibration data in different view. The texture images and calibration datawhich are folded in subfolder named with Arabic numerals.

D_R	- rotation matrix in binary, repesents the SLR camera pose relative to industrial camera
D_T	- translation matrix in binary, repesents the SLR camera pose relative to industrial camera
kc_right	- distortion parameter
kk_right	- intrinsic camera parameter matrix
RT.txt	- RT matrix in different views. view 1 is identity matrix
